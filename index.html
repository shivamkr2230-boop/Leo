Our Campus

School Facilities

A safe, modern, and stimulating campus designed to support every stage of your child's growth and curiosity.

Add Photo

Computer Lab

Our well-equipped computer lab introduces young learners to basic digital literacy through fun, age-appropriate activities. With modern systems and guided sessions, children build confidence with technology in a safe and supervised environment.

Add Photo

Science Lab

A hands-on discovery space where curiosity comes to life. Children explore simple experiments, observe everyday science concepts, and develop early reasoning and observation skills under the guidance of trained teachers.

Add Photo

Playground

Our spacious, secure playground is designed for active, joyful play. With safe, age-appropriate equipment, children build strength, balance, and coordination while learning to share, take turns, and make friends.

Add Photo

Biology Lab

A dedicated space where students explore the wonders of living things through models, specimens, and guided observation. Hands-on activities help children understand plants, animals, and the human body while nurturing a lifelong curiosity for life sciences.

Add Photo

Chemistry Lab

Equipped with safe apparatus and supervised by trained staff, our chemistry lab lets students perform simple, age-appropriate experiments. It builds a strong foundation in scientific method, safety habits, and the joy of discovery.

Add Photo

Library

A quiet, welcoming space filled with books for every age and interest. Our library encourages a love of reading, independent learning, and research skills, helping children grow into curious, lifelong learners.

A Message for Our School Family

At Green Field Academy, we are committed to providing a world-class early education that builds a strong foundation for lifelong learning. Our mission is to create a joyful, safe, and inclusive environment where each child's unique strengths are celebrated every single day.

We believe the earliest years are the most critical for cognitive, social, and emotional development. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to make every day at GFA a meaningful journey of discovery, wonder, and growth.
